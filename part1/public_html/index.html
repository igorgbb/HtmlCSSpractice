Minecraft


Description

Minecraft is an open world game developed by Mojang, played by millions of players worldwide. In the game players take the role of Steve, and have to gather resources to build houses and battle monsters called mobs. The most popular mob is the Creeper, of which can explode and cause damage to the player.
